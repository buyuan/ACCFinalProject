Warner (1881–1958) Producer Harry M. Warner was born on December 12, 1881 in Krasnoshiltz, Russian Empire as Hirsz Mojzesz Wonsal. He was a producer, known for The Lost City (1920), My Four Years in Germany (1918) and Speed (1919). He was married to Rea Ellen Levinson. He died on July 25, 1958 in Hollywood, California, USA.
