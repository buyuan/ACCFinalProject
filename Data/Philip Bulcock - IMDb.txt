Trivia: Philip Bulcock has collaborated with director Nic Saunders four times over an almost ten year period. In each film, Bulcock plays the lead role and each is enigmatically named to never reveal their true identity - The Cowboy, The Poet, The Pianist and The Hitchhiker.
