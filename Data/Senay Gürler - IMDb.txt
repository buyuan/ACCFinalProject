She graduated from Dokuz Eylül University's Fine Arts faculty. She performed at the Izmir Art Theatre and worked at TRT as a presenter. After working as a dubbing artist, she began acting in films and television. She is best known for playing Fatos in the comedy series Avrupa Yakasi. Open ...
