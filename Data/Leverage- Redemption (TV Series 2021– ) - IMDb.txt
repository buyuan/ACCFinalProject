core characters, including Harry Wilson, played by five-time Emmy nominee Noah Wyle, and Breanna "Bree" Casey, played by Aleyse Shannon. They are joined by original cast members Beth Riesgraf reprising her character as Parker, Gina Bellman as Sophie Devereaux, and Christian Kane as Eliot Spencer, who will all be series regulars. Aldis Hodge also returns as Alec Hardison in a recurring role, subject to availability from his Showtime series City on a Hill, whose Season 2 will likely be filming at the same time as this series.
