He is best know for Ain't Them Bodies Saints (2013), Pete's Dragon (2016), and A Ghost Story (2017). At the age of 19, Lowery wrote and directed his first film, Lullaby. Lowery had his breakthrough with his first feature film, St. Nick (2009). He often works with Casey Affleck.
