There are a number of similarities to Child's Play (2019). Both movies were made by 20th Century Fox/Studios. Both Andy and Barney are lonely kids with no friends who find friends through their experiences with the toys. Both kids have a missing parent. Both kids are given the hottest new toy that is malfunctioning. Neither toy is purchased legally. Neither toy can properly connect to their company's network. Both toys have similar errors in their speech. Both kids realize something is wrong with their toys after it physically hurts someone or something despite normal versions being restricted against it. Both toys upload their code to others so they can act the same way. Both toys are apparently lost in the end with a tease that they could return. Both Chucky and Ron are voiced by actors (Mark Hamill and Zach Galifianakis respectively) who voiced Joker in Batman animated movies. 12 of 12 found this interesting Interesting?
