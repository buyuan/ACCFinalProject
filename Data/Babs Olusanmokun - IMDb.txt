Working in film and on stage, he is known for Black Mirror (2017), The Defenders (2017), The Night Of (2016) and Roots (2016). Born in Lagos/Nigeria, he is multilingual, fluent in English, Portuguese, French and Yoruba. He is a third degree Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu black belt. He lives in New York.
